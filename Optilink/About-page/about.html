OUR STORY


Once upon a time at NBI School of Tech, five wannabe frontend developers - Alina, Jacob, Liam, Fredrik, and Anders - embarked on a final project. Their idea? Smart lenses they named "OptiLink." It was a blend of fashion, AI, and tech, but they had no idea what they were doing.

In their cluttered discord chat, they built these lenses with more duct tape than coding. Miraculously, it worked! Alina strutted down a holographic runway, flaunting AI fashion advice. Their presentation wowed everyone, and OptiLink was born. Investors flocked, and they became accidental tech moguls, swimming in cash.

The Fab Five laughed at their absurd journey, turning a school project gone wrong into a global sensation that changed tech and fashion forever.
